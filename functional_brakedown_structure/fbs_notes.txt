
*** Mirror cleaning Readiness & Start conditions/procedure ("55.GA UWAVES Instrument and
    Control Archetecture", page 42):
    
    1) Receive flag from Diagnostic Control group (D1) to participate in upcoming mirror cleaning.
    
    2) 55.GA executes a sequence to get ready.

        2a) Open Bullnose shutter.

        2b) Load required settings into all RF components.

        2c) Setup monitoring of forward and reverse power at the RF matcher.

    3) D1 monitors gase pressure inside vacuum vessel.

    4) Once pressure inside vessel is nominal, D1 sends trigger to start cleaning.

*** Mirror cleaning Stop conditions/procedure ("55.GA UWAVES Instrument and Control Archetecture",
    page 42):

    Mirror cleaning can be stopped or paused at any time. It can be stopped if excess reverse power
    is detected. Or, under normal operation it will stop after a predetermined amount of time.
